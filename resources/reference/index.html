OM2Csound is a library for the control of Csound synthesis from OpenMusic. It is consituted of a 'score' editing part, an 'orchestra' editing part and a general synthesis feature. Score and orchestra editing tools are mainly low level modules for formatting Csound instructions and writing .sco and .orc files to be read by Csound. The synthesis feature (CSOUND-SYNTH) requires Csound to be installed and declared as an OM external (see Preferences/Extrernals). Csound can also be selected as a sound normalizer in OM sound processing (see Preferences/Audio).
